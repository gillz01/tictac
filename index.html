VARAN'S TIC-TAC-TOE

Welcome to Varan's Tic-Tac-Toe game! To play, you're going to need 2 players.
Each of you will take turns pressing the cursor on one of the boxes; player 1 will take all odd turns such as turns 1, 3 , and 7, while player 2 will take all even turns such as turn 2, 4, 6!
When you click a square, you will leave a symbol, either X or O. The first one you get three of their symbols in 1 row is the winner. Click the Restart button to try again! Click the button below to get started!

Player 1, you will be going on every odd turn, which will be numbers ending with 1, 3, 5, 7, 9. Please pick which icon you want to use; Sun or Moon. Player 2 will be using the opposite icon. If you select Play Game without choosing an icon, player 1 will default to Sun and player 2 will be Moon.
